My Travel Blog

Nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, Darjeeling enchanted me with its lush tea gardens, colonial charm, and breathtaking vistas. My journey began with a picturesque drive and an iconic Toy Train ride. I witnessed a mesmerizing sunrise at Tiger Hill , explored tea estates, savored local delicacies, and immersed myself in the vibrant culture at Chowrasta.Visits to the Darjeeling Zoo and the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute added a touch of adventure and history to my trip. As I bid farewell to this serene hill station, I left with a heart full of memories and a deep appreciation for its natural beauty.
